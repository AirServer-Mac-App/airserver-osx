## What is AirServer?

**AirServer** is a high-performance **screen mirroring and streaming utility** that transforms your Mac into a universal receiver. Supporting **AirPlay, Miracast, and Google Cast**, it enables users to display or record screens from multiple devices simultaneously — without cables or additional adapters.

With **AirServer Mac**, your computer becomes the center of collaboration. You can mirror iOS devices for app demos, show presentations wirelessly, stream gameplay, or share videos in classrooms, offices, and creative studios. It’s designed for reliability and clarity, offering **crisp 4K visuals** and **real-time responsiveness** with minimal latency.

Unlike basic mirroring tools, AirServer includes **session recording, adaptive scaling, and security options** like on-screen passcode pairing, ensuring controlled and private streaming sessions. It works perfectly across **Apple Silicon (M1/M2)** and Intel Macs, maintaining stable performance even during multi-device mirroring.

The latest version, **AirServer 7 for Mac**, offers optimized performance for **macOS Ventura and Sonoma**, supporting seamless AirPlay integration and smooth streaming to external displays or projectors. Whether you’re using a **MacBook**, **iMac**, or **Mac mini**, AirServer ensures effortless content sharing from all your devices.
